facAIlizer
The facAIlizer program will detect and draw a rectangle around the faces of users present within the camera's vision, at which point it will begin determining the Gender and Emotion of each face detected in real time, displaying the info on the top of each rectangle --so that the relevant info is attatched to to the respective user-- and displaying the confidence levels as a bar graph for the first user.
